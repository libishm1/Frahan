# Loviisa KB11 surface fracture map (colocated subset)

Self-contained subset for example 26: one outcrop (KB11) of the Loviisa rapakivi-granite manual
fracture-trace map. Style: short sentences, no em dashes.

Files (ESRI Shapefile sets, companions required by NetTopologySuite):
- `KB11_tulkinta.{shp,shx,dbf,prj,cpg}` - 708 manual fracture-trace polylines (Finnish tulkinta =
  interpretation).
- `kb11_area.{shp,shx,dbf,prj,cpg}` - the mapped-domain boundary polygon.

Source: Chudasama 2022, Loviisa rapakivi-granite fracture-trace dataset, DOI 10.5281/zenodo.7077494,
CC-BY 4.0. CRS EUREF_FIN_TM35FIN (EPSG:3067), units metres. Full 8-site dataset:
`../../../data/loviisa/`.
